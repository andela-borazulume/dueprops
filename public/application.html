DueProps

My name is Obie Fernandez and I'm the CEO and Founder of DueProps.com.

I had the idea for DueProps while I was running my own web design and development agency. It turned out that even when you have a great company culture, it's hard to keep employees happy and motivated unless you make them feel appreciated every day.

But how do you get yourself and your people to remember to give thanks to each other every day? Turns out the secret is not that complicated. You just need a system. But not any old system will do. It needs to be fun, and the tokens of appreciation have to be memorable, so that it doesn't take a lot of time to figure out what you want to say. In fact, with most props, just a word or two of description will suffice.x
